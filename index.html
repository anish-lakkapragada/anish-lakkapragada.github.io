hey there! I’m an undergraduate at Yale University double majoring in computer science and mathematics. I am currently focused on using statistics to understand machine-learning models. Over the past few years, some research areas I have explored include out-of-distribution (OOD) detection, multi-task learning, and basic neural network optimization. I have had the privilege of presenting research at CVPR 2024, NeurIPS 2023, AAAI 2023, and Databricks Data+AI Summit 2022. To date, I have 50 citations.

Additionally, I love building software to reach thousands. I have written a few notable Chrome Extensions, specifically one for accessibility and another for Yale/USC. I also have experience leading and contributing to much larger full-stack platforms. If you have an idea, please reach out.

In my free time, I enjoy reading and photographing birds.
