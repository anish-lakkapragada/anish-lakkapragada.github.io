  <h1>tubestack</h1>

  <p class="tubestack-lede">
    tubestack turns youtube videos into quiet, readable articles. search for something, open a result, and read the transcript without the feed pulling you somewhere else.
  </p>

  <p>
    it is a small desktop app for reading youtube with more intention: read later, history, themes, and a lock mode for when you actually want to finish the thing you opened.
  </p>
